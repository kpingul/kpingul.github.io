NahamCon: Offensive Security Conference 2023

I'm writing to share my first time experience attending NahamCon 2023, a virtual offensive security conference.

What is NahamCon?

NahamCon,founded by @NahamSecis an event that brings together a diverse group of security professionals, hackers, and enthusiasts covering a wide range of OffSec related topics such as bug bounty hunting, red teaming, OSINT, and a bunch more. The conference consists of CTFs, workshops, presentations, and held online over a 3 day period.

Highlights

The conference was hosted by @_johnhammond, @ippsec, and @Alh4zr3d who did a great job in keeping things live and engaging throughout the days​. The conference covered a bunch of topics, like strategies for effective bug bounty programs, insights into leaked secrets, building automated attack surface recon, how to own APIs for valid submissions, and a ton more. The session that really blew me away was about hacking root EPP servers to take control of DNS zones. And at the end, there was a fascinating discussion about how GPT-based AI will disrupt security. It was like getting a sneak peek into the future of what's to come in this space.

Summary

NahamCon was a fun experience, packed with enlightening sessions that highlighted the importance and ever-evolving nature of offensive security. If you're into OffSec, you should definitely check it out next year. And lastly, a huge shout out to NahamSec, the hosts, presenters, and everyone that was involved in making this such an awesome event.
